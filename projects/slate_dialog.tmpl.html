Slate

C# . UnityScript . Unity . Photoshop

This was my major project when I worked at Christie Digital. Slate is a multiplayer Unity game mapped on a tetrahedral (read: trianglular pyramid) with three projectors. I made a capture-the-flag mode with various mechanics including mines, freeze, and shield. You and up to 3 friends each control a small ship with your smartphones.

Login Screen

Connecting to the local network with your smartphone gets you to this screen, where you can choose your ship.

Smartphone UI

Directional controls on the left and points, health, ammo count, and current mechanic on the right. It was Christmas time, so every little present box on the map gave you a completely random mechanic!
