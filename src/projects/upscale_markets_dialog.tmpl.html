UpscaleMarkets

HTML5 . CSS3 . Javascript . Angular . git

An group exercise in conducting a complete business analysis, hashing out the requirements, designing the software architecture, and making a prototype demo of a grocery shopping web application.

Shopping Cart

The shopping cart shows the current balance and the breakdown of each item in the cart. It also shows the user the number of points they could earn by completing the transaction.

Use Cases

Only a few of the customer use cases were completed for this prototype. These include:

  • Shop
  • Manage Profile
  • Manage & View Rewards
